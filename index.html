About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Julia Sheremet! I'm originally from the sunshine state and moved to Portland to transition from a SEO Specialist to a Frontend UI/UX Developer. I'm fascinated by the intersection of art and technology and strive to fashion avant-garde designs for user interfaces that look good and work even better.

At Epicodus, the React/User Interface/JavaScript track gave me the resources to discover fundamental technologies, tools, and resources to create unique user experiences. I hope to work with innovative brands that pioneer bolder online experiences. Aside from my work, I'm an avid coffee drinker that loves to travel, enjoys yoga, and painting.
